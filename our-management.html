Management Team


Mrs. Olufunmilayo Shobowale

Exec. Assistance (Founder's Office)

Mrs. Olufunmilayo Shobowale

Mrs. Olufunmilayo Shobowale , a professional of note, is the Executive Assistant (Founder’s Office). She had her Secondary School Education at Acadington School, Oxford, United Kingdom and later proceeded to Leicester University, Leicester, United Kingdom where she obtained a Second Class Upper Division, Bachelor of Science Degree in Chemistry with Business Management.

She later completed her professional accounting examinations in December, 2007 and became an Associate of Association of Chartered Certified Accountants (ACCA) in the United Kingdom. She worked in various establishments in the United Kingdom: first at Toyota Great Britain, Epson where she superintended the finance and fund income function, then at EDF Energy, Gatwick and later at Mitsubishi, London. Since returned to Nigeria and took up appointment first with Chapel Hill as an Investment Advisor before joining Elizade in 2009.

Mr. Emmanuel Oluwasayo Oluwaniyi

Head, After-Sales

Mr. Emmanuel Oluwasayo Oluwaniyi

Pastor Emmanuel Oluwasayo Oluwaniyi, HEAD, AFTERSALES (HAS) holds a B.Sc. (Hons.) Degree in Accounting from the University of Benin, Benin City, Nigeria and a Master of Business Administration with specialization in Marketing. He is a Fellow of The Institute of Chartered Accountants of Nigeria (ICAN); Member, Nigerian Institute of Management (Chartered) and an Associate Member of the Chartered Institute of Taxation of Nigeria (CITN). A seasoned Business Administrator, he has over 26 years’ professional experience in Manufacturing, Finance, Management Consultancy, etc., in different organizations and in various sectors of the Nigerian economy.

Mr. Oluwaniyi, who has attended several training courses locally and internationally also served on the Governing Board of the Newspapers Proprietors’ Association of Nigeria in a representative capacity and was the Financial Controller of Audit Control and Expertise Lagos, Nigeria, a subsidiary of ACE International with Headquarters in Geneva, Switzerland, before joining the Elizade Group in 2005.

He is happily married with children.

Mr. Sunday Ojonimi Aiyegba

Head, Human Resources and Administration

Mr. Zaheed Adisa Ajibade

Mr. Oladunni Odeyinka

Company secretary / Head, Legal and Corporate Affairs

Mr. Oladunni Odeyinka

Mr. Oladunni Odeyinka, Head, Legal and Corporate Affairs, is a Solicitor and Advocate of the Supreme Court of Nigeria. He is a graduate of the University of Benin with Honours Degree in Sociology and Anthropology. He also has Honours degree in Law from the Lagos State University. He, thereafter, attended the Nigerian Law School and was called to the Nigerian Bar in 1990. He obtained an Associate degree (AACM) from the Indian Christian University, U.S.A. in 1999. He also attended many courses both locally and internationally. He has wide and varied experience in various organizations including Salesmanship Universal Business Machines (Minolta); and Vanguard Newspapers Limited. He was Head of Chambers, Rotimi Sanni & Co. (Legal Practitioners), Company Secretary/Legal Adviser of Innovative Mortgage Finance Limited; amongst others. He joined Elizade Nigeria Limited in 2008 as the Head, Legal & Corporate Affairs. He is happily married with children.

Mr. Akeem Olatunde Adisa

Head, Information Communication Technology

Mr. Akeem Olatunde Adisa

Mr. Akeem Olatunde Adisa, Head, Information and Communication Technology, holds a B.Sc. (Hons) Degree in Industrial Mathematics with specialization in Computational and Numerical Analysis. He did his Post Graduate Studies in Computer Sciences at the University of Benin, Benin City, Nigeria and an MBA (Finance & Accounts) from the University of Ibadan, Ibadan, Nigeria. He is a Certified Microsoft Professional (MCP) and also a Microsoft Certified Technology Specialist.

Mr. Adisa is a Professional Member of the Computer Professionals Registration Council (CPRC) and Nigeria Computer Society (NCS) with over 25 years professional experience garnered in Manufacturing, Technology, Research, Oil and Gas Sectors of the economy. He has attended several professional courses locally and internationally. He is married with children.
