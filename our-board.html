Our Board


Chief Michael Ade. Ojo

Founder

Chief Michael Ade. Ojo, Founder/Director, studied Business Administration at the University of Nigeria, Nsukka, Nigeria, where he met his late wife and was one of the pioneer students of the University.

Chief Michael Ade-Ojo

Chief Michael Ade. Ojo, Founder/Director, studied Business Administration at the University of Nigeria, Nsukka, Nigeria, where he met his late wife and was one of the pioneer students of the University. He graduated in 1965 with a Second Class Upper Degree. He worked with CFAO Motors, Federal Civil Service and British Petroleum [BP] before leaving to set up the company. The name ELIZADE evolved from an inspiration triggered by the nuptial union with his late wife Elizabeth Wuraola.

ELIZADE was coined from the first four letters of his wife’s first name ‘Eliz’ and his middle name ‘Ade’. Together they passionately nurtured a strong vision, and this vision has been the driving force of the company’s success over the years. Today, the Company has grown from a single company to a Group of many companies.

Chief Ade.Ojo’s exceptional entrepreneurial skill is clearly exhibited in his position as Chairman of various companies in Nigeria, like Toyota Nigeria Limited, Moorhouse Sofitel Limited and Custodian Insurance & Allied Plc etc. He has also been on the Board of so many successful companies at various times including, among others Owena Motels Limited, Eterna Oil and Gas Limited and AP Ltd. He is a Philanthropist and has contributed tremendously to the well being of the Society and the under-privileged. He single handedly embarked on, and completed a major community development project, the 8.5km Ilara Mokin township roads, Ondo State in Nigeria.

He is also established the Elizade University based at Ilara-Mokin, Ondo State which is aimed at giving the average Nigerian child quality education. Chief Ade.Ojo’s immense contribution to the development of the society has earned him numerous awards locally and internationally. He is also a proud recipient of the prestigious Nigeria’s National Award of Officer of the Order of the Niger (OON) and is also a Knight of John Wesley.

Dr. Herbert Ademola Ajayi

Chairman

Dr. Herbert Ademola Ajayi

Dr. Herbert Ademola Ajayi was appointed Chairman of the company in 2007. He holds a Doctorate Degree in Engineering Management and worked with the United Africa Company, UAC where at different times he served as Northern Area Service Engineer, Northern Area Manager and Lagos/Western Area Manager, responsible for Company's operations in Lagos/Western Region of PAN Electric Limited (UAC) Nigeria.

Dr. Herbert Ademola Bertley Ajayi, (OON) is currently the National President of Nigerian Association of Chambers of Commerce, Industry, Mines and Agriculture (NACCIMA) a seasoned Administrator, and a Lighting Expert. Dr. Ajayi, an icon in Nigeria’s Organized Private Sector (OPS) for some three decades.

Dr Ademola Ajayi holds several awards and honours including the Distinguished Industrialist Award; Institute of Corporate Administrators Award; Dedicated Humanitarian Service Award amongst others. He is a fellow of the Institute of Directors, FIoD, Institute of Corporate Administrators (FICA) and the Institute of Arbitrators. He is also a proud recipient of the prestigious National Award of Officer of the Order of the Niger (OON). Dr. Herbert Ademola Bertley Ajayi is a Christian of the Catholic Faith and married with children.

Mrs Adeola A. Sagoe

Vice Chairman

Mrs Adeola A. Sagoe

Adeola A. Sagoe (Mrs), an astute business-woman and internationally acclaimed Fashion Designer. Is the vice-chairman. She holds a Bachelor’s Degree in Business Administration from the University of Miami, United States of America and an M.Sc. (Finance) from the University of Lagos, Akoka, Lagos, Nigeria.

Mrs. Sagoe, who has been a Director of the company since 1992, is on the Board of several other companies.

Mr. Demola Ade-Ojo

Managing Director

Mr. Demola Ade-Ojo

Demola Ade-Ojo (Managing Director) is a consummate Business Administrator and financial expert. He is a graduate of Business Administration, University of Florida, Gainsville, Florida, United States of America. He holds a Master of Business Administration (MBA) degree from Wayne State University, Detroit, Michigan.

He had varied working experience in the United States of America which include his stint with Toyota Motor Sales, Incorporated (TMSI), Torrance, California, USA where he served in several capacities as e-Business Manager, Market Researcher, Customers’ Relation Manager, Toyota Quality Sales Process Initiator, Coordinator, Toyota Product Model Year Review, Reviewer of Toyota National Dealership Incentive and Marketing Programmes, etc. He is also credited with the development of the business transition model from the traditional finance to direct on-line finance through a unique partnership strategy with Toyota Dealers. He also worked as a Licensed Financial Planner and Stockbroker with Tabron Financial Services in Detroit, Michigan, USA, Sales Consultant/Credit Analyst with Mel Farr Ford, Oak Park, Michigan.

Demola, who, before his departure to the United States of America and his eventual return, served in various capacities at Elizade Nigeria Limited, before becoming its Managing Director in January 2005. He was the pioneer Business Development & Strategic Planning Head of the company and played a pivotal role in broadening the Corporate Vision, Mission of the company and in the development of its products and business strategies. He is a Director in Toyota Nigeria Limited, 3-Line Card Nigeria Limited and several other companies in Nigeria. He is a man of integrity and a homely personality who is happily married with children.

Mr. Bola Akingbade

Director

Mr. Bola Akingbade

Bola Akingbade, Director, a graduate of the then University of Ife (now Obafemi Awolowo University) was one of the pioneer Students in the then newly-created Department of Food Science and Technology. He holds a Master of Science Degree in Bio-Chemical Engineering with distinctions in Biochemical Reaction Engineering and Biochemical Engineering Process Design of the University College, London.

On his return to Nigeria, Mr. Bola Akingbade became an Assistant Lecturer in Food Engineering. He was also awarded an (MBA) with distinctions in Quantitative Analysis and Marketing Management. His work experience saw him occupy various Management positions in the following blue-chip organizations: Food Specialties Nigeria PLC (now Nestle Nigeria PLC.); Nigerian Breweries PLC and MTN Communications Nigeria Limited. A committed and erudite Marketing Professional, Bola was also decorated with awards of Lifetime Achievement Award (Publishers of Marketing World); Fellow, of the Nigerian Institute of Marketing; Fellow, Advertising Practitioners’ Council of Nigeria; . He is married to Mrs. Baderinwa Akingbade and are both blessed with children.

Mr. Abel Omodele Ajayi

Director, Finance

Mr. Abel Omodele Ajayi

Mr. Abel Omodele Ajayi is the Executive Director (Finance) of the Company. He completed his professional accounting examinations in December, 1971; a Fellow of The Institute of Chartered Accountants of Nigeria (ICAN); Fellow of Association of Chartered Certified Accountants (ACCA, UK); and an Associate of The Chartered Institute of Taxation of Nigeria (CITN).

He was appointed a Partner in Peat Marwick Ani Ogunde & Co., a predecessor firm of KPMG Nigeria effective January 1979, and assumed full responsibility for the Audit and Accounting arm of the practice following his election by his peers as Partner-in-Charge, Audit and Accounting in January, 1993.

His areas of specialization include Consumer Business, Manufacturing Business, Banking and Finance, and he advised most of the leading companies in those industries. He was elected Chairman/Chief Executive of KPMG Nigeria in September 2002, a position he held until the merger of KPMG Nigeria and Akintola Williams Deloitte & Touche in June 2003. He served as Vice Chairman in the merged Firm, Akintola Williams Deloitte until 31 May, 2008.

He had during his almost 30 years as a partner in two of the top four global professional services organizations attended development seminars/workshops in Switzerland, UK, Australia, Hong Kong, France, Germany, Singapore, Netherlands, Belgium, South Africa, Kenya, Canada and USA. He joined Elizade Nigeria Limited in November 2008 as Group Financial Adviser and was appointed Finance Director in November 2009. He is happily married with children.

Mr. Olu Tikolo

Director, Marketing

Mr. Olu Tikolo

Mr. Olu Tikolo is the Executive Director (Marketing) of the Company. He has a B.Sc. (Hons) Economics Degree and a Post Graduate Diploma in Financial Management from the University of Ibadan. An Alumnus of Said Business School, University of Oxford, he also has attended several Management Training Courses in Nigeria and Abroad.

Mr. Tikolo is a Fellow of the National Institute of Marketing of Nigeria, Fellow of the United Commercial Travelers Institute, fellow of the united commercial travelers institute, Fellow of the American Marketing Association and Member of several other Management and Marketing Associations. He is the Chairman of the Automobile and Allied Industries Sector of the Transport Group of the Lagos Chamber of Commerce; eight time winner of the Toyota Marketing Manager of the Year Award and the Best Automobile Marketing Manager (all brands) in Nigeria in 2006.

He joined the Elizade Group in May 1998 as the General Manager, Marketing and became Director, Marketing in May, 1999. He is happily married with children.
